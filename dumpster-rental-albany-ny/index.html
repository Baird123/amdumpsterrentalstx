Frequently Asked Questions

What size dumpster do I need for a home renovation?

For typical renovations, a 10-20 yard dumpster suffices, but our experts can help determine the ideal size based on debris volume.

Are there items I cannot put in the dumpster?

Yes, hazardous materials such as chemicals, paints, batteries, and certain types of electronics cannot be accepted.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Rental periods are flexible; our team will customize timelines to match your project needs.

Can I schedule same-day delivery?

In most cases, yes. Contact us early in the day for same-day delivery options.

Do you assist with placement and permits?

Yes, our staff guides placement and can advise if any local permits are required for your dumpster.

What’s included in your pricing?

All costs include delivery, pickup, disposal fees, and rental duration. We provide upfront quotes with no hidden charges.
